Tramonto

About

Tramonto ("Sunset") is a charming, family-owned Italian restaurant in Florence, known for its warm ambiance and stunning evening views. Inspired by the golden Tuscan sunsets, Tramonto blends traditional recipes with fresh, seasonal ingredients.

Hours:

  • Mon–Sat: 12:00–10:30
  • Sun: Closed

Location

Via delle Rose 18, Florence, Italy
